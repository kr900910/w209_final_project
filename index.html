Legalization of Cannabis: Its Financial and Other Social Impacts


In recent years legalization of recreational cannabis has become a point of national debate. Cannabis use has been already proven to have medical benefits, but political and industry leaders have made various claims on its financial and social impact, not all of which are entirely fact based.

Our goal is to enable users to explore the impact of Cannabis from three perspectives:

  • Financial: the economic impact to a state and its economy
  • Public Health: focusing on the impact on the opiate related deaths
  • Crime: the effect on crime statistics

DATA:
The data is collected from individual state websites and data sources. The data is cleaned and preprocessed for better visualization.

Does cannbis legalization have any impact on crime rates?

In this section, you can explore whether cannabis legalization has any impact on crime rates by looking at historical marijuana-related crime data in Denver.

Navigation Instructions:

  • The first visualization shows the time series of marijuana-related crime incidents in Denver. If you hover over the category name on the right, you can filter a specifc crime category.
  • The second visualization shows percentage of each crime category in which you can analyze whether a specific crime has increased over time compared to other crime categories.
